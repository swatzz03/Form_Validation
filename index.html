Course Title: Fundamentals of AI

Instructor: Dr. Emily Carter

Duration: 8 weeks

Description: This course offers a broad introduction to artificial intelligence, covering essential concepts such as machine learning, neural networks, and robotics. Students will explore the history, current trends, and future prospects of AI, while gaining hands-on experience with AI tools and platforms. Through practical assignments and case studies, participants will learn how AI is applied across various industries. This course is ideal for anyone interested in understanding the basics of AI and its potential impact on society.

Start Date: October 15, 2024

Course Title: Data Analytics with Python

Instructor: Dr. Michael Lee

Duration: 10 weeks

Description: This course dives into the fundamentals of data analytics using Python, one of the most popular programming languages in the field. Students will learn how to collect, clean, and analyze data using Python libraries like Pandas, NumPy, and Matplotlib. The course includes real-world projects to apply data analytics techniques, making it ideal for aspiring data analysts and anyone interested in data-driven decision-making.

Start Date: October 22, 2024

Course Title: Natural Language Processing (NLP)

Instructor: Dr. Sarah Johnson

Duration: 12 weeks

Description: This course provides an in-depth exploration of Natural Language Processing (NLP), a critical area of AI that focuses on the interaction between computers and human language. Students will learn about text processing, sentiment analysis, and machine translation, using popular NLP libraries like NLTK, SpaCy, and Transformers. Through hands-on projects, participants will build NLP models that can perform tasks such as text classification and language generation.

Start Date: November 1, 2024
